// notes
  • Tax bands for England, Wales & Northern Ireland 2025/26. Does not cover Scottish rates.
  • Personal allowance is £12,570, tapering by £1 for every £2 earned above £100,000.
  • Salary sacrifice reduces gross pay before tax and NI are calculated.
  • Benefits in Kind increase taxable income but are not deducted from cash pay (P11D value).
  • NI: 8% on £12,571–£50,270, then 2% above £50,270.
  • Bonus is taxed at your marginal rate based on total annual income including bonus.
